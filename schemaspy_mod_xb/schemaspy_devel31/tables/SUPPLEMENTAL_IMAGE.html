Table DEVEL31.DB2INST1.SUPPLEMENTAL_IMAGE
Some images will have supplemental versions. This may be a larger version of the image. If the image submitted had to be reduced in size, resolution or had to have its format changed the submitted image may be stored as a supplemental image.
